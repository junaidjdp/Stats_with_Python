\paragraph{Tables, Histograms, Boxplots, and Slicing for
Statistics}\label{tables-histograms-boxplots-and-slicing-for-statistics}}

When working with a new dataset, one of the most useful things to do is
to begin to visualize the data. By using tables, histograms, box plots,
and other visual tools, we can get a better idea of what the data may be
trying to tell us, and we can gain insights into the data that we may
have not discovered otherwise.

Today, we will be going over how to perform some basic visualisations in
Python, and, most importantly, we will learn how to begin exploring data
from a graphical perspective.
